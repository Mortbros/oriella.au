Dr Oriella Cattapan is a clinical psychologist, forensic psychologist and family therapist who has worked with children, adolescents and adults for over 20 years. Dr Cattapan has worked extensively in child and adolescent mental health, adult mental health, adult drug and alcohol, government and non-government sectors. Dr Cattapan's practise is informed by family therapy paradigms, as well as psychodynamic and cognitive behavioural. Dr Cattapan strongly adheres to a belief that each person is unique and treatment must be tailored to suit their unique characteristics.
Dr Cattapan understands that seeing a psychologist is a deeply personal experience. Having confidence in the psychologist is of paramount importance. To feel that the psychologist is able to understand the presenting problem and has the ability to be of assisance, at what is a very difficult time, is essential.

Dr Cattapan's regular session times are by appointment Monday to Friday.

Dr Cattapan has many areas of expertise and is happy to receive your call to discuss whether she is the most suitable practitioner to help you address the issues you are facing.
